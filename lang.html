Language Options

Welcome to Covicometer! We strive to make our platform accessible to users around the globe. To better serve our diverse audience, we offer our website in multiple languages. Select your preferred language below to access real-time COVID-19 data and statistics in the language most comfortable for you.

Available Languages

  • English
  • Español (Spanish)
  • Français (French)
  • Deutsch (German)
  • 中文 (Chinese)
  • 日本語 (Japanese)
  • 한국어 (Korean)
  • Italiano (Italian)
  • Português (Portuguese)
  • Русский (Russian)
  • العربية (Arabic)

    • How to Change Language

  • Desktop Users:
    • - Click on the language dropdown menu located at the top right corner of the website. - Select your preferred language from the list.
  • Mobile Users:
    • - Tap on the menu icon in the top right corner.
    - Scroll down to the language options.
    - Choose your preferred language.
