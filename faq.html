Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What is Covidometer?

    • CoviCometer is a platform that provides real-time data on COVID-19 cases, recoveries, and deaths, broken down by country. Our goal is to deliver accurate and up-to-date information to help you stay informed during the global pandemic.

  • Where does Covidometer get its data?

    • We aggregate data from reputable health organizations and official government reports to ensure the highest level of accuracy and reliability. Our sources include the World Health Organization (WHO), Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), and various national health departments.

  • How often is the data updated?

    • Our data is updated in real-time as new information becomes available from our trusted sources. This ensures that you have access to the most current statistics.

  • How can I use the data provided by Covidometer?

    • You can use CoviCometer to stay informed about the spread of COVID-19 in different countries, track trends over time, and make data-driven decisions for personal safety or policy-making.

  • Is Covidometer free to use?

    • Yes, CoviCometer is completely free to use. We believe that access to accurate information is crucial during this pandemic and strive to make our platform available to everyone.

  • How do I navigate the website?

    • Our website features a user-friendly interface that allows you to easily access data by country. Use the search bar or navigate through our menus to find detailed statistics and trends for specific regions.

  • Can I trust the data on Covidometer?

    • Yes, we ensure that all data on Covidometer comes from trusted and verified sources. We are committed to providing reliable and accurate information to our users.

  • How can I contact Covidometer for more information or support?

    • For any inquiries or feedback, please contact us at [info@covidometer.com](mailto:info@covidometer.com). Our team is here to assist you and answer any questions you may have.

  • Does Covidometer provide information on COVID-19 variants?

    • Yes, we provide information on COVID-19 variants when available from our sources. This includes data on the spread and impact of different variants in various regions.

  • Can I contribute data or report discrepancies?

    • We welcome contributions and reports of discrepancies. If you have verified data or notice any inconsistencies, please contact us at [info@covicometer.com](mailto:info@covicometer.com) so we can review and update our information accordingly.
    Stay informed and stay safe with Covidometer. Thank you for trusting us as your source for COVID-19 data.
